How it works

Bedade aggregates data from experts for your use. You know your industry, the data you need, and who has the right knowledge. Bedade is a platform to exchange that knowledge. You choose your target contributors, type your question and watch the results roll in. Get better data quicker. Extract data from within your network to make comparisons and benchmarks, instead of relying on guestimates or expensive data sources. Anonymously shared data from your trusted contacts will provide insight for your critical decisions. View the Diagram
